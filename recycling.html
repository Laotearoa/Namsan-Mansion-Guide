House Guide
Waste & Recycling
Korea has a strict recycling policy. Thank you for your cooperation — it makes a real difference.
🗑️ Trash Bins: Main bins are located in the kitchen. Small bins in the rooms are for temporary use only — please transfer to the kitchen bins regularly.
🛍️
General Waste
일반 쓰레기
🖨️
Printed / Patterned Bag
Use the bag with printed designs or images
For non-recyclable items that don't fit other categories.
Rinse food containers before disposal to reduce odour.
♻️
Recyclables
재활용 쓰레기
🫙
Clear, Unprinted Plastic Bag
Plain transparent bag — no prints or patterns
Bottles, cans, plastics, cardboard, glass.
Rinse before disposal. Clean items recycle better and prevent contamination.
🌽
Food Waste
음식물 쓰레기
🟡
Yellow Bag
Designated yellow food waste bag
For edible food scraps only — vegetable peels, leftovers, fruit flesh.
No bones, shells, pits, or fruit seeds. These go in general waste.
Seal the bag tightly and place in the small white bin provided.
📄
Paper Waste
종이류
Stack clean, dry paper — newspapers, cardboard, paper bags.
Bundle or place in a bag or box and leave outside for collection.
Do not mix wet or food-stained paper — this goes in general waste.
🌙
Trash Collection Time
By 7:00 PM

Place sealed trash bags by the sidewalk near the Pole before 7 PM. Trash is collected at night. Do not leave bags out during the day.

⚠️ Using the wrong bag type or mixing waste incorrectly can result in fines under Korean law. When in doubt, message us and we'll help.
