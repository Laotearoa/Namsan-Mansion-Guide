Appliances & How Things Work
All appliances are pre-set and ready to use. This guide explains what each button does if you'd like to make adjustments.
🌡️
Floor Heating & Hot Water · Rinnai RBMC-59
Boiler Controller
✅The boiler is already set to a comfortable temperature. No action needed — just enjoy.
Button Reference
전원 & 잠금 · Power & Lock
Press to turn on/off. Hold 3 seconds to lock or unlock all buttons (child lock).
난방 · HEATING
Activates floor heating (ondol). Press again to turn off heating while keeping hot water available.
온수 · HOT WATER
Activates hot water supply. Can be used independently from floor heating.
Left + / −
Adjusts floor heating temperature. Typical range: 40–60°C.
Right + / −
Adjusts hot water temperature.
절약 · ECO
Energy saving mode. Limits maximum temperature to reduce gas usage.
예약 · SCHEDULE
Timer function — set the boiler to turn on/off at a specific time.
실온/온돌 · Mode
Hold 3 seconds to switch between room temperature control and ondol (floor heating) mode.
💡Left display = current room temp · Right display = set temperature.

Cooling & Heating · LG System A/C
Air Conditioner Remote
Button Reference
전원 · POWER
Turns the unit on or off. Point remote at the indoor unit.
운전 선택 · Mode
Cycles through modes: Cool → Heat → Fan → Dry.
온도 · Temperature
Opens temperature setting. Use < > to raise or lower.
바람 세기 · Fan Speed
Changes fan speed: Low → Medium → High → Auto.
상하 바람
Up/down airflow direction. Hold 3s for auto swing.
좌우 바람
Left/right airflow direction. Hold 3s for auto swing.
바람 선택
Switches air circulation / ventilation mode.
파워 바람
Power Wind — maximum fan speed for rapid cooling or heating.
나가기 / 밝기
Back / exit menu · Brightness adjusts display light level.
Button Reference
전원 · POWER
Turns the unit on or off.
운전 선택 · Mode
Cycles through modes: Cool → Dry → Fan → Heat → Auto.
온도 조절 ∧ / ∨
Raises or lowers the target temperature one degree at a time.
바람 세기 · Fan Speed
Changes fan speed: Low → Medium → High → Auto.
상하 / 좌우 바람
Adjusts airflow direction up/down and left/right.
파워 바람
Power Wind — maximum fan speed.
자동 건조
Auto Dry — runs fan briefly after shutdown to prevent mould.
쾌적 절전
Comfort Save — balances comfort and energy efficiency automatically.
실내 온도
Displays current indoor temperature on the remote screen.
잠금 · Lock
Locks all buttons to prevent accidental changes.
꺼짐 예약
Sleep timer — sets the unit to turn off automatically after a set time.
켜짐 예약
On timer — sets the unit to turn on automatically at a set time.
설정 해제
Cancels any active timer or scheduled function.
💡Each room has its own A/C unit and remote. Check the bedside table or the holder near the unit.

Samsung Bespoke AI · All-in-One
Washer / Dryer
✅Load laundry, select a cycle, press Start. Detergent is pre-loaded — no need to add anything.
Button & Control Reference
전원 · POWER
Turns the machine on or off.
건조기 · Dryer
Selects dryer mode (upper door). Turn the dial to choose a drying cycle.
세탁기 · Washer
Selects washer mode (lower door). Turn the dial to choose a wash cycle. 세탁+건조 washes and dries in one go.
Mode Dial
Turn to select a cycle. Recommended: 세탁+건조 (Wash + Dry) — no transfer to dryer needed.
건조기 ▶| · Dryer Start
Starts or pauses the dryer cycle. Door locks automatically once started.
세탁기 ▶| · Washer Start
Starts or pauses the washer cycle. Door locks automatically once started.
Dry Level · 건조 모드
Adjusts drying level: More Dry / Normal / Less Dry / Air Dry.
Wrinkle Prevent · 구김 방지
Tumbles clothes periodically after the cycle ends to prevent creasing.
More Options · 부가 기능
Extra settings: Extra Rinse, Bubble Soak, Delay End, and more.
🧴Detergent drawer: Located on the right side of the washer. Open it to find two compartments — left for detergent, right for fabric softener. Detergent is pre-loaded, but you can refill as needed.
💡When the cycle finishes, the door opens automatically. No need to move clothes to a separate dryer.
🧹Dryer lint filter: After using the dryer, please remove lint from the front filter on the dryer door. This keeps the machine running efficiently and safely.
⚠️Use HE (High-Efficiency) detergent only if you add your own. Regular detergent causes excess suds and errors.

Coffee Machine · Nespresso Vertuo Plus GCB2
How to Brew
☕Complimentary capsules are in the kitchen. Help yourself — a range of intensities are available.
Quick Guide
Power button
Top of machine. Press once to turn on — turns steady green when ready (~20 sec).
Lever up (open)
Opens the capsule head. Insert capsule dome-side down — machine reads barcode automatically.
Lever down (lock)
Locks the head. Push firmly until it clicks before brewing.
Brew button
Press once to start. Blinks while brewing → steady green when done. Press again to add extra volume.
Lever up (eject)
Ejects used capsule into the container below. Always eject before inserting a new one.
Auto-off
Turns off after 9 minutes. Manual off: push lever down and hold 3 seconds.
