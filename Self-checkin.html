Troubleshooting
Odd numbers (1·3·5·7·9) flash with beeping
This is a mechanical error signal — the deadbolt motor has detected a jam or obstruction. Do not keep pressing buttons. Try gently pushing the door flush against the frame while pulling the handle again. If it persists, message us on Airbnb.
⚠️
Wrong code entered 5 times in a row
The lock sounds an alarm and disables all input for 3 minutes. Do not press anything during this time. After 3 minutes, the lock resets automatically — tap the screen and try again carefully.
🔴
Keypad doesn't light up at all
The battery may be fully discharged. Please message us on Airbnb straight away and we will resolve it as quickly as possible.
🔋
Still can't get in
Message us on Airbnb — we will get back to you as soon as we see your message and resolve it as quickly as possible.
